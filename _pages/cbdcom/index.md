<h4>November 10–13, 2026 - Melbourne, Australia</h4>
<hr/>

<p style="text-align:justify">The IEEE International Conference on Cloud and Big Data Computing is a premier forum for researchers, practitioners and developers who are interested in cloud computing and big data to explore new ideas, techniques and tools, as well as to exchange experience. Besides the latest research achievements, the conference covers also innovative commercial data management systems, innovative commercial applications of cloud computing and big data technology, and experience in applying recent research advances to real-world problems.</p>

<p style="text-align:justify">
IEEE CBDCom 2026 will be the 12th edition of the conference after the success of CBDCom 2015 in Beijing, CBDCom 2016 in Toulouse, CBDCom 2017 in San Francisco, CBDCom 2018 in Guangzhou, CDBCom 2019 in Fukuoka, CDBCom 2020 in Calgary, CDBCom 2021 held virtually, CDBCom 2022 in Calabria, CDBCom 2023 in Abu Dhabi, CBDCom 2024 in Boracay Island, and CBDCom 2025 in Hakodate, Japan. It will continuously offer a platform for researchers to exchange novel studies, discuss important issues and explore key challenges in innovative cloud and big data for smarter world. IEEE CBDCom 2026 will be held on November 10–13, in Melbourne, Australia, co-located with IEEE CyberSciTech 2026, IEEE PICom 2026, and IEEE DASC 2026. It aims to bring together computer scientists, engineers and researchers from academia and industry to discuss and exchange experimental and theoretical results, work-in-progress, novel designs, and future trends in cloud and big data innovations.
</p>
